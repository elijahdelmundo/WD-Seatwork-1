Snorlax

Elijah Miguel S. Del Mundo

BSIT CST - TC05

Welcome to the Snorlax fan page! Snorlax is a large, bipedal, bear-like Pokémon that is primarily blue-green with a cream-colored face, belly, and feet. Its eyes are usually closed, giving the impression that it is always asleep. Snorlax is known for its immense appetite and its tendency to sleep almost constantly, only waking up to eat.

Explore the various sections using the navigation buttons above to learn more about Snorlax, view a gallery, or get in touch!

Snorlax Abilities

  • Immunity: Prevents poisoning.
  • Thick Fat: Halves damage from Fire and Ice-type moves.
  • Gluttony (Hidden Ability): Allows the Pokémon to eat a held Berry when its HP drops to 50% or less.
